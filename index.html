About Me

I'm a Master of Software Engineering student at Yoobee Colleges, passionate about creating thoughtful, inclusive technology. I’m building skills in Python and C++, with a growing focus on Digital Asset Management to support the effective organization and accessibility of digital content. My interests lie in projects that blend cultural awareness, creativity, and user-centered design. Whether I’m refining technical language, designing intuitive interfaces, or structuring digital systems for long-term impact, I aim to make tech feel human—and genuinely useful.


Outside of coding, I’m a musician. Music keeps me grounded, connected, and inspired—it’s where collaboration and creativity come alive in a different form.
